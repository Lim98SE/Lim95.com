Broadway Breakdown 4

is a platformer game for PC, Mac, and Linux. It is the fourth game in the Broadway Breakdown series of games. Broadway Breakdown is a primarily Scratch-based series, though the creator, Lim95, has moved onto GDevelop 5, a much more powerful engine than Scratch.


What is BB4?

It's a platformer! A simple little run-and-jump game where you travel through 16 worlds of 4 levels each! This thing is twice as long as the original Super Mario Bros!
